Q: What does the with command do in Python?

A: Creates a context-manager, which performs cleanup after exiting the adjacent indented block.


Q: What different modes, passed as arguments to the open() function, are there for opening a file in Python?

A: Read-mode('r', the default mode), Write-mode('w'), and Append-mode ('a')
